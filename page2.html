Do What Assignment

-For our first assignment, we had to create a list of instructions and bring in 3 examples.

-We then had to give the instructions to a classmate and observe their finished work.

-These are the instructions:

1. Grab a pen and a piece of paper

2. Draw 3 small to medium V's in a line, touching at the tops.

3. Now draw a zig-zag line about a quarter away from the bottom of each V- within the boundaries of each V.

4. About a half an inch below one of your V's, draw a circle that is smaller than your V's.

5. Fill in the circle.

6. Flip page over.
